Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Q: How do I find my remote's frequency?

A: Look for a sticker on the back of your remote or check your user manual. Common frequencies are 300, 315, 318, and 390 MHz.

Q: Will this work with my garage door opener?

A: This tool is specifically designed for Chamberlain, LiftMaster, and Craftsman models from 1995-2015. Check the compatibility table above.

Q: Is the conversion permanent?

A: No, this doesn't modify your remote. It helps you find the compatible frequency for programming.

Q: Do I need special tools?

A: No special tools required. Just your remote and this converter.

Troubleshooting Tips

Remote not responding after conversion:

  • Verify the original frequency matches your remote
  • Check remote battery
  • Ensure proper programming sequence

Inconsistent operation:

  • Clear any metal objects between remote and receiver
  • Try reprogramming the remote
  • Check for interference from nearby devices

Programming difficulties:

  • Hold remote closer to the garage door opener
  • Follow programming sequence exactly
  • Try fresh batteries
